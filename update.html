What's New

Version 2.0 (June 16, 2014):

2.0 was an entire rewrite.

  • New keyboard shortcut for your convenience. Just use Ctrl + Right Arrow to bring up the popup.
  • New higher resolution icon.
  • You are now able to save your own presets via the options page. Just click the gear icon.
  • The first 10 presets are hotkeyed to Ctrl + 1-0 once the extension popup is open.
  • The inverse of all your presets are available by adding Shift to the keyboard shortcut. ie. Ctrl + Shift + 1 will resize the window to the opposite of preset one (height becomes width, and width becomes height, think landscape vs. portrait).
  • Various bug fixes.

How to Use

  • Ctrl + Right Arrow - opens the extension popup
  • Up - manually increases browser width
  • Down - manually decreases browser width
  • Ctrl + 1-0 - resizes to saved preset
  • Ctrl + Shift + 1-0 - resizes to the landscape/inverse of saved preset
  • Ctrl + Esc - resets browser window to 1200x800

Cmd is substituted for Ctrl on Mac.


NOTE: Because Cmd + Shift + 3 and Cmd + Shift + 4 on a Mac take a screenshot, landscape or inverse orientarion for the devices in position 3 and 4 won't work on a Mac.
